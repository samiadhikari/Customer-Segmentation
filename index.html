About Us

We are a customer segmentation platform. We help you carve your customers. Due to a large diversity of products, their features and sales makes it difficult to find the pattern of customer's preferences. Our project knuckles down how the customer makes purchasing patterns and divides them into segments on which we can enable the businesses for their profitability by retaining and gaining customers.

We help you visualize your customer base through generating clusters which helps you make strategic decisions accordingly.
